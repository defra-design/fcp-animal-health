    Guidance

    Vet summary templates for all species

    Access the vet summary templates for all species. These should be filled in as part of an animal health and welfare review or endemic disease follow-up.

    From:
    Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs
    Published
    26 June 2025
    Last updated
    27 August 2025 — See all updates

    Applies to England

    Contents

    1. Cattle vet templates
    2. Pig vet templates
    3. Sheep vet templates
    4. Details

    These vet summary templates should be filled in as part of an animal health and welfare review or endemic disease follow-up. The information on the summary will be used by the farmer when making a claim.
